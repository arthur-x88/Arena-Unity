# Fairy - The Enchanted Sprite
## Intelligence Hero

### Base Stats (Level 1)
- **Strength**: 17 (+1.7 per level)
- **Agility**: 18 (+1.6 per level)
- **Intelligence**: 25 (+2.7 per level)
- **Health**: 150 (+1.7 * 18 health per level)
- **Mana**: 0 (No mana system)
- **Armor**: -0.52
- **Magic Armor**: 5.5
- **Movement Speed**: 295
- **Attack Range**: 550 (Ranged)
- **Attack Damage**: 20-27
- **Attack Speed**: 1.7 seconds cooldown

### Primary Role: Nuker/Support
Fairy is a high-scaling Intelligence hero who specializes in fairy magic and enchantment. Her abilities focus on area control, healing, and magical disruption.

### Ability Build Guidelines

#### Core Abilities (Priority Order)
1. **Enchanted Dust** (Level 1) - Primary damage ability
   - Deals damage and applies magical effects
   - Strong harass and poke tool
   - Excellent for weakening enemies

2. **Fairy Ring** (Level 2-3) - Ultimate area ability
   - Creates a magical circle with various effects
   - Active: Deals damage and provides utility
   - Core of Fairy's versatility

3. **Pixie Dust** (Level 4, 6, 8) - Mobility and utility
   - Increases movement speed and provides evasion
   - Great for positioning and escapes
   - Strong defensive tool

4. **Fairy Fire** (Level 5, 7, 9) - Sustained damage ability
   - Deals damage over time with magical burn
   - Excellent for prolonged fights
   - Strong attrition tool

#### Recommended Ability Progression
**Early Game (Levels 1-6)**: 1, 2, 1, 3, 1, 2
**Mid Game (Levels 7-12)**: 2, 3, 2, 4, 2, 4
**Late Game (Levels 13-16)**: 4, 3, 4, 1, 4, 3

### Gameplay Guidelines

#### Early Game (0-15 minutes)
- Farm safely from range using Enchanted Dust
- Use auto-attacks for last-hitting
- Position for safe farming with your range
- Look for opportunities to use Pixie Dust

#### Mid Game (15-30 minutes)
- Start grouping with team for objectives
- Use Fairy Ring for area effects
- Position for team fight initiation
- Build up damage with Fairy Fire

#### Late Game (30+ minutes)
- Become the primary damage dealer
- Use Fairy Ring for team fight utility
- Focus on eliminating key targets
- Use Enchanted Dust for constant pressure

### Team Synergy
- **With Supports**: They sustain you while you deal damage
- **With Initiators**: Provide follow-up damage after engages
- **With Carries**: Protect them while they farm

### Counterplay Awareness
- **Vulnerable to**: Anti-magic effects, high physical damage
- **Strong against**: Clumped enemies, low-mobility heroes
- **Watch out for**: Magic immunity, heavy physical damage

### Advanced Tips
1. Use Enchanted Dust to stack magical debuffs
2. Position Fairy Ring for maximum area coverage
3. Time Pixie Dust for defensive plays
4. Use Fairy Fire for sustained damage pressure
5. Master enchantment timing for maximum impact
